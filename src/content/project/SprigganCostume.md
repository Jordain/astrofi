## Summary

Inspired by face off the TV Series. With the help of [Dallas Harvey](https://vancouverfx.com/). This is my first time tackling a prosthetic appliance. I spent a number of months working on this since this was my first time and I was learning how to mold, sculpt, bake and paint. I spent a long time finding the right concept design for my creature. I came up with about 15 different designs before I chose this one. I wanted to create something that kept the human features that allow you to emote with it. I also wanted to create a design where your eyes get drawn in and follows the flow of the design to go out and back in.
